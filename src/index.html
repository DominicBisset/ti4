Join me some time this Christmas/New Year period, TBA, to play an eight-hour Space Opera

Key Details:

  • Where: My House
  • When: Don't know yet - probably 26th Dec->3rd Jan
  • What time: Doors open 10am, aiming to start playing by 10:30am.

I own Twilight Imperium. Its an eight-hour, six player game. This is not something you pull out casually after dinner. This takes organisation.

The event

I'll be running a game some time late December or early Jan. If you've been linked to this page, I want you to join me. I need five dedicated souls willing to simulate a galaxy with me. Even if you're not that dedicated, you're still welcome to drop by throughout the day and spectate and keep Hannah company. Perhaps you'll catch the Twilight Imperium bug and want to be first in the queue for the next game.

Bring your own lunch. We might have a short interlude to eat, but it won't be long enough to go shopping. We can order takeway or similiar in the evening if we're still going.

If you're in, jump down to Register Interest. Otherwise read on and learn a bit more.

What am I getting myself into?

If you're not immediately disgusted at the concept of spending an entire day pretending to be diplomatic space tortoises, perhaps look one or more of the following videos by my Games Reviewers of choice Shut Up and Sit Down. They explain it far better than I ever can.
