Let's Play Twilight Imperium
Round Three

Join me on the 9th of November 2019 to play an eight-hour Space Opera

Key Details:

  • Where: My House
  • When: Saturday, 9th of November
  • What time: 10:30am sharp
  • Places are given out on request and on commitment of reading the rules, being on time, and acting according to the spirit of the game

I own Twilight Imperium. Its an eight-hour, six player game. This is not something you pull out casually after dinner. This takes organisation.

The event

I'm running a game, starting 10:30 the morning of the ninth of November 2019. If you've been linked to this page, I want you to join me. I need five dedicated souls willing to simulate a galaxy with me. Even if you're not that dedicated, you're still welcome to drop by throughout the day and spectate and keep Hannah company. Perhaps you'll catch the Twilight Imperium bug and want to be first in the queue for the next game.

Bring your own lunch. We might have a short interlude to eat, but it won't be long enough to go shopping. We can order takeway or similiar in the evening if we're still going.

If you're in, jump down to The Commitment. Otherwise read on and learn a bit more.

What am I getting myself into?

If you're not immediately disgusted at the concept of spending an entire day pretending to be diplomatic space tortoises, perhaps look one or more of the following videos by my Games Reviewers of choice Shut Up and Sit Down. They explain it far better than I ever can.

The Commitment

I understand if you're not interested in spending an entire day pretending to be an alien race. It's not for everyone. Its a long time to have a bad time for, so don't be afraid to say it's not for your. On the assumption that I'm going to get a lot of "no thanks", I've invited a lot of people and places will be given out on a first-come-first-served basis. If the five spaces fill up I'll still take a waiting list in case people drop out.

So how do I get a place I hear you ask? To get a place, you need to respond to me and make the following five declarations:

Yes, I'm pretty serious about this. I want this to go well. I eagerly await your response.

Alternatively, just turn up and spectate. It'd be nice to know if you're planning on turning up, when, and how long for though, just so I know when to expect you. Maybe you could play some smaller board games in the background if enough people are interested.
